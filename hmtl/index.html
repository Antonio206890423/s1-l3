Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brue. It will become your new favorite holyday starter!

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1 1/4 extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

Method

1.Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix togheter all the dough ingredients until the flour is fulli absorbed. You don't need to actually jnead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
